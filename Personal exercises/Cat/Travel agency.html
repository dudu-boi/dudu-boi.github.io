Welcome to Dodgy Destination

About Us:

Ever felt like your life is a tedious cycle of work, bills, and pretending to enjoy small talk? Fear not! At Dodgy Destinations, we specialise in whisking you away to mysterious (read: suspiciously cheap) locations where you can "find yourself"—or at least lose your wallet in a back alley.

Our Services

  • Budget Breakdowns – Because who doesn’t love last-minute panic when hidden fees appear?
  • Exotic Getaway – Experience culture by ordering the only dish you recognise on the menu.
  • Luxury Travel – Pay premium prices to sit in an aircraft seat that still barely reclines.
  • Mystery Trips – We pick a place, you pray it has plumbing.

Why Choose Us?

Because TripAdvisor is for cowards. You don’t need reviews—just blind faith and an adventurous stomach. We guarantee an unforgettable experience… mostly because trauma is hard to forget.
